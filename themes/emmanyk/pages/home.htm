Call for Applications

The call will run for six weeks to encourage participations across the country; the web portal will provide the platform for all submissions and subsequent evaluations. Experts trained by Enterprise Lab and other relevant partners will deploy the marking scheme to evaluate the concepts and rank them accordingly.

Cohort Selection

The top 25 ideas will participate in a 3 days boot camp to redefine their offering, tweak their model where possible and refine their business model canvas. The top 10 ideas will be admitted in the 10 weeks programme.

Investment Pitch

The investment pitch is in the final week of the 10 weeklong programmes, with the outcome of selecting the top 5 ideas that will receive funding.
